Sisters Uncut

"2 women a week are killed by a partner or ex-partner in England and Wales alone"

Who

Sisters Uncut

What

Sisters Uncut are a collections of groups of women in London taking direct action for domestic violence services.

They meet weekly on Thursdays evenings in different parts of London. Their work includes protests, petitions, letters to MPs and social media campaigns. For example, blocking bridges across the country in November 2016 in response to the government considering reducing domestic violence services.

Where

Sisters Uncut started in London, but encourage the formation of new local groups across the UK.
